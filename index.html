The Power of SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website so it appears higher in search engine results when people search for products or services online. By optimizing content, keywords, website structure, and performance, SEO helps search engines understand what a website is about and recommend it to users. SEO is important for businesses because it increases online visibility, brings more potential customers to their website, and helps them compete in the digital marketplace without relying only on paid advertising.

Why the TikTok Algorithm Is Different

Unlike other platforms where posts are mainly shown to followers, TikTok pushes videos to the "For You Page" (FYP) where they can be seen by massive audiences regardless of follower count. It analyzes watch time, interactions, and trends. Because it relies heavily on short-form video and frequent posting, managing TikTok requires dedicated video production and a unique strategy, separating it from standard image-based social media management.
